# 2025 US Citizenship Test Prep

An Android app designed to help LEGAL immigrants prepare and practice for the U.S. Citizenship test
to obtain the U.S. Citizenship based on the [U.S. Citizenship & Immigration Services Naturalization 
Test and Study Resources](https://www.uscis.gov/citizenship/find-study-materials-and-resources)

## Prologue 

My father is waiting for his U.S. Citizenship test appointment. Hopefully, he receives the 
appointment long after I ship a working version of this app to enhance his performance so I can brag
about making my dad a citizen like god made Adam and Eve.
